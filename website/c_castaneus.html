C. castaneus
Virginia Wesleyan University
Camponotus castaneus
Information


The full name of this species is the Camponotus castaneus. This species of ant usually resides in dirt and underneath stones. This species of ant is very timid and usually nocturnal. Their timidness allows them to be victims of the invasive species in the region. These ants help the biodiversity rather than hurt it because they are very timid.
