Information


This graph shows the total number of ants in each area that data was gathered in, and the total number of invasive vs noninvasive species collected. This shows us that the noninvasive species heavily dominate the invasive species in the Hampton Roads and greater regions. As seen on the data prediction webpage, invasive populations can multiply fast and can quickly overpower the noninvasive species in an area. This is why the data and knowledge of invasive species, and not just for ants, should be monitored and shared publically.
