Formica subsericea
Information


The full name of this species is the Formica subsericea. This ant species is very timid, but they build extremely fast. They can replicate nests and build vast nests in a very short period of time. Once they move to a region they take little to no time to full inhabit the region. A species like this would be invasive, but they are timid and do not attack other types of bio diversity in the region.
